Speaking at PyData PDX Meetups

Have you been working on an interesting project that you want to show off? Found a really cool library that you want everyone to know about? Whether it’s a half-hour slot or a 5-minute lightning talk, get in touch and let’s get you on the stage!

There’s a few details to note:

  • Each meetup will have two longer talks — 30 minutes talk time (including any Q&A)
  • There is time for two or three 5 minute lightning talks
  • Check the NumFOCUS Code of Conduct — all language and imagery must be inclusive (e.g. prefer something like ‘folks’ over ‘guys’)
  • No product pitches — we’re strict about this — these aren’t sales slots and the room is not populated by buyers
  • Your talk should educate, but you’re welcome to note "we’re hiring" or similar during your talk

To submit a talk, visit the members section of PyData PDX Meetup page and get in touch via “Contact” button. Send the organisers a message and one of us will get back to you ASAP. Please make sure to include: a title, a short abstract and an email address.
